About Us

Discover Our Story

Opened in April of 2003, Umamichi is the dream and vision of Iha Takeo to have an establishment that is both hip and authentic with a refreshing atmosphere high in energy and dedication. His dream lives on, for in his memory this restaurant has retained its integrity through the sole proprietorship of Teruya Takeo, a local who you can find kicking back daily with the loyal patrons.

We're committed to serving authentic Japanese cuisine to the community. We pride ourselves in preparing our dishes with fresh, quality ingredients. We offer a variety of traditional Japanese delicacies and entrées, as well as an array of crafty sushi rolls. Our fine selection of beers, sakes, and wines complement the sophisticated flavors of our menu items.
